Parsonnet

Sophie Parsonnet, born in 2000, is an emerging multidisciplinary artist who grew up in Atlanta, Georgia. Sophie is currently pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in Studio and Fine Arts while minoring in Creative Technology and Design at the University of Colorado Boulder, working with multimedia arts, painting and drawing but prodominately in digital media. She uses the feelings of happy and sad in many of her pieces as she is an advocate for the ever-changing feelings many people that deal with mental health problems face. Sophie has interned with startup company, Popmenu, based out of Atlanta, GA, graphically designing restaurantsâ€™ social media platforms. In December of 2022, Sophie had her first exhibit at IRL Gallery in Denver, Colorado where she utilized the program of Augmented Reality. She has also contributed over 300 hours of community service with the National Charity League around her hometown. In college, Sophie has been an active member of Chi Omega, Beta Chapter. Through Chi Omega, she volunteers her time to give back to the Make A Wish Foundation Colorado. Sophie currently is based in Boulder, Colorado and in her free time, she enjoys seeing the beautiful Colorado scenery whilst snowboarding and taking in the mountainous views.
